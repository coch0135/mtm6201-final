MISSION

Cultivate the holistic welfare of neglected pet animals.

At Saint Francis Pet Refuge, we believe that every pet animal deserves a healthy and fulfilling life. Our Adoption Coordinators vigorously screen potential homes for safety and suitability, and follow up with pawrents to offer guidance and resources supporting the adoption.

HISTORY

We’ve been helping animals in need since 2020.

Saint Francis Pet Refuge was founded in Ottawa by Kelly Francis Cochran in the early days of the Covid pandemic, when the supply of neglected pets and the demand for companion animals skyrocketed. As word spread, our organization expanded across Ontario.

LOCATION

Our state-of-the-art facility houses our residents in style.

Every animal housed in our facility has a private indoor kennel with regular access to bedding, toys, and treats. Dogs enjoy large, securely fenced outdoor spaces, while cats socialize in an elaborate playroom. Adopters and pets get to know each other in cozy meeting rooms.
